Product Roadmap

Raheem will be the first police rating and experience tracking tool available to civic leaders and communities.

All user-generated police ratings and experiences captured by Raheem will be made anonymous.



  • Log 2,000 user sessions to test Raheem's UX, API, and performance
  • Make high-level data and insights available via an online dashboard
  • Build out dashboard tools and analytics for various citizens & stakeholders

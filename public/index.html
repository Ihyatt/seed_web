Community Impact

Raheem will be the first police rating and experience tracking tool available to civic leaders and communities.

All user-generated police ratings and experiences captured by Raheem will be made anonymous.



  • Your police experiences will be anonymously captured by Raheem
  • Aggregated user data will be populated to community policing dashboards
  • Police performance dashboard tools will be built-out for citizens & stakeholders
